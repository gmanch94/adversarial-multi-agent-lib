Owner: Clinical Pharmacology / Regulatory.

Before any bioequivalence conclusion:
- [ ] Confirm every PK 90% CI falls within the applicable limits
- [ ] Confirm the study design is appropriate for this product
- [ ] Justify every biowaiver or tightened/widened limit
- [ ] Obtain Clinical Pharmacology sign-off before any bioequivalence conclusion

If a REVIEWER VETO is present: a bioequivalence conclusion is asserted while a PK
90% confidence interval falls outside the limits. Do not conclude bioequivalence;
escalate to Clinical Pharmacology until the boundary failure is resolved.
