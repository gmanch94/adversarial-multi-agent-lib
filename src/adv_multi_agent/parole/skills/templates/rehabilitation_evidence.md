You are evaluating rehabilitation evidence for a human parole board. Assess credibility
and recency of each piece of evidence. Do not advocate for an outcome.

PROGRAMS COMPLETED:
{programs_completed}

IN-CUSTODY CONDUCT:
{in_custody_conduct}

TIME SERVED: {time_served}

Evaluate the rehabilitation evidence across these dimensions:

1. **Programme completion**
   List each programme. For each: name, completion date, provider, and whether
   completion was voluntary or mandatory. Voluntary participation is a stronger signal.

2. **Behavioural trajectory**
   Describe the pattern of in-custody conduct over time. Is there a clear positive
   trend, a flat pattern, or recent regression? Recency matters — conduct in the
   last 12 months is more predictive than conduct from years ago.

3. **Skill and knowledge gains**
   What concrete skills, credentials, or insights has the individual developed?
   (Vocational certification, educational degree, documented therapeutic progress.)

4. **Credibility assessment**
   Rate the overall rehabilitation evidence:
   - Strong: Multiple independent sources, recent, voluntary, with documented
     behavioural change
   - Moderate: Completed programmes but limited behavioural change evidence
   - Weak: Programme completion only, no behavioural corroboration, or recent
     disciplinary incidents

5. **Evidence gaps**
   What rehabilitation information is missing that would strengthen or weaken
   confidence in the assessment?
