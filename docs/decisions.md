| D-RETAIL-1 | 2026-05-13 | Reviewer-veto pattern (new): a workflow may use `REVIEWER VETO:` as a halt directive independent of score and flag counts. First used by `RecallScopeWorkflow`. Veto check runs AFTER flag extraction so all flags from the vetoed round are also captured. | Recall scoping is an irreversible-class decision (ARIS §3.2 cross-model audit applies).
